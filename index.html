Clown Town Society

Welcome to Clown Town, where the 10,000 funny alter-egos of the original CryptoPunks gather to dress up and entertain the Ethereum crowd. All metadata and images are fully generated and stored on-chain.

CryptoPunk owners can mint the randomly generated clown version of their punk for free. During public sale, anyone can mint clowns for unclaimed punks, but half the proceeds are automatically transferred to the corresponding CryptoPunk owners. Cost of minting depends on whether you mint a clown from a human punk (0.08E), from a zombie (0.2E), from an ape (0.4E) or from an alien (0.8E). 10% of all profits are donated to GiveDirectly.org in contract.
